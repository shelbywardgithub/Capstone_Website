Different Type of Cybersecurity Threats

What is Malware?

Malware

Malware is a malicious sofware such as spyware,ransomware, viruses and worms.

Malware is activated when a user clicks on a malicious link or attachment,which leads to installing dangerous software.

What is Phishing?

Phishing

Phishing is when attacks use fake communications,such as email , to trick the receiver into opening it and carrying out the instruction inside,such as providing a credit card number.
